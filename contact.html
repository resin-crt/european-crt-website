Information

The European Climate Risk Typology has been developed within the RESIN project – Climate Resilient Cities and Infrastructures . This project received funding from the European Union’s Horizon 2020 research and innovation programme under grant agreement no. 653522.

Contact Details

The European Climate Risk Typology was developed at the University of Manchester, with the support of the University of Sheffield. The following staff were involved in the development of the typology, all of whom work at the University of Manchester aside from Stephen Hincks who is at the University of Sheffield:

  • Dr Jeremy Carter (Principal Investigator)
  • Dr Stephen Hincks (Co-Investigator)
  • Mr Vasileios Vlastaras (Researcher)
  • Dr Angela Connelly (Co-Investigator)
  • Professor John Handley (Co-Investigator)

Citation
