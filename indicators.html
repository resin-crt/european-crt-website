Describing the typology.

This section will include details of each of the indicators used to develop the Climate Risk Typology. The indicators will be described, source data will be provided and in cases where the indicator has been developed specifically for the typology the methodology applied to create the indicator will be described. Issues linked to the use and interpretation of each indicator in the context of climate risk will also be included here.
